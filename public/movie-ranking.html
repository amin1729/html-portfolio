The Best Movies to Watch According to Amin

My top 3 movies of all time

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

The best psychological love thriller that I have ever watched.

Blinded by the Light

One of the nice-est feel good movies of all time. The soundtrack is beyond amazing!

The Pursuit of Happyness

Happiness with a 'y'. One of the best biopics of all time. Truly inspirational.
